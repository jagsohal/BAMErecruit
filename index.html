OUR PROMISE

Fingers Crossed

BME Recruitment is a new recruitment agency focused on increasing the number of Black and minority ethnic individuals in employment throughout the UK . We take a holistic approach offering guidance on health & well-being issues through to employment matters .We support in finding supply work, long term roles, permanent positions, apprenticeships and volunteer opportunities within all sectors.

 

We connect BME Senior leaders in order to promote engagement strategies and work as positives influencers.

 

As an agency we adhere to 'The Equality Act 2010' and take what the law terms 'positive action' to help employees or job applicants that:

​​are under-represented in the organisation, or whose participation in the organisation is disproportionately low because of a protected characteristic

BAME Senior leaders 

Our recruitment of experienced BME senior leaders offers employers the chance to recruit individuals with proven track records.

1. to aim to increase the numbers of BME individuals within senior positions

2. to provide employers with valuable individuals, that will act as leaders and decision makers within their organisations.
